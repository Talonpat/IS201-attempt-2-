Talons Upcoming Travels

The next plan that Talon Has is to drive from the Top of Alaska down to the bottom of Argentina. Talon Will complete this journey in a group of 6 people. They will be taking 2 1st gen Toyota Tacomas and a Porshe Cayenne.

This trip will not be done in all one go. Although it is possible and only takes about 4 months, Talon and the people he is going with have responsibilites that they have to attend to back here in the USA. This trip will be broken up into segments over the course of 2 years. Below is the list and the date of the segments that they are going to take.

  1. Alaska -> Salt Lake City
    • August 2024
  2. Salt Lake City -> Mexico City
    • October 2024
  3. Mexico City -> Panama City
    • April 2025
  4. Panama City -> Lima Peru
    • June 2025
  5. Lima Peru -> Antofagasta Chile
    • August 2025
  6. Antofagasta Chile -> Buenos Aires Argentina
    • December 2025


Did you know that Talon has done this trip before? When Talon was 14 or 15 he did part of this trip from Santiago Chile to Lima Peru as part of the Expeditions 7 World Wide expedition.
